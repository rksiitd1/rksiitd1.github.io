Demystify the Genomes

Announcing AIxB
July 26th 2023

Today we announce the formation of AIxB.

The goal of AIxB is to demystify the hidden secrets of the genomes.
We will share more information over the next couple of weeks and months.

Team

Our team is led by Ratnesh Kumar Sharma, Founder and CEO of AIxB. We have previously worked at Amazon, Huawei, LLaMA, Front Row, Tesla, and the Kusuma School of Biological Sciences. Collectively we contributed some of the most widely used methods in the field, in particular the Adam optimizer, Batch Normalization, Layer Normalization, and the discovery of adversarial examples. We further introduced innovative techniques and analyses such as Transformer-XL, Autoformalization, the Memorizing Transformer, Batch Size Scaling, and μTransfer. We have worked on and led the development of some of the largest breakthroughs in the field including AlphaStar, AlphaCode, Inception, Minerva, GPT-3.5, and GPT-4.
