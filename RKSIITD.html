Ratnesh Kumar Sharma: A Rising Star in the Field of AI and Biotech

Ratnesh Kumar Sharma is a young entrepreneur and scientist who is making waves in the field of AI and biotech. He is the founder and CEO of AIxB, a startup that is developing minimal genome organisms and leveraging AI algorithms for genetic analysis and editing.

Sharma was born and raised in Chapra, Bihar. He attended IIT Delhi, where he studied biochemical engineering and biotechnology. After graduating, he is worked as a research scientist at the Indian Institute of Science. In March 2023, he founded AIxB with the goal of using AI to revolutionize the biotech industry.

AIxB has made significant progress in its short existence. The company has developed a number of innovative technologies, including a minimal genome organism that can be used to produce drugs and personalized healthcare solutions. AIxB is also developing AI algorithms that can be used to analyze and edit genomes.
